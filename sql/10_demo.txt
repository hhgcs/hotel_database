purpose:

demoing the complete flow for the system.

flow:
1) client creation (with encrypted data)
2) reservation creation
3) automatiom of room assignment
4) client check-in
5) room state change to "Occupied"
6) service reservation
7) generation of service charge
8) client check-out
9) generation of room charge
10) payment processing
11) reservation state change to "Finished"
12) room state change to "Cleaning"
